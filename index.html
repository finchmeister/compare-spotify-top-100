Compare Spotify Top 100 Playlists

Spotify give you your top 100 playlists each year, compare them to discover how your music taste has evolved throughout the years. Using this tool, you'll gain an insight into your most played tracks, artists, and albums for each year.

You'll need:

  • To have retained two most recent Spotify Top 100 playlists
  • Be on the desktop version

If you don't have 2 consecutive top 100 Spotify playlists, it's cool, hit the 'demo me' button below to see how this thing should work.

Step 1:

First you gotta find your playlists. In Spotify, search for your top 100 playlist from last year.

Step 2:

Make sure the playlist is in the correct order, then select the first track of the playlist and ctrl + a, ctrl + c to copy all the ordered Spotify track Ids to your clipboard.

Paste them into the box below:

Step 3:

Find your current top 100 playlist.

Copy all the tracks to the clipboard again and paste them into the box below:

Compare that shit.

About/Credits

I didn't build this to change the world, I built this because I didn't get the 'your year in music' email from Spotify. I wanted to know what my year in music was and how it had changed from last year's. If they weren't going to tell me, I was going to find out myself - now you can, too. Here's what I used:

Bootstrap v4 • front-end framework for handling the colouring in
spotify-web-api-js • client-side JS wrapper for the Spotify API
Unsplash • an awesome place for free images
GitHub Pages • for hosting
